LOG NGAY 29.04 - ConversationServiceImpl
1. Tong quan thay doi

Da hoan thien service xu ly conversation thay vi de cac method rong. Conversation service hien co the:

- Lay danh sach conversation summary, sap xep theo updatedAt moi nhat.
- Lay chi tiet mot conversation kem danh sach member.
- Tao conversation PRIVATE hoac GROUP.
- Cap nhat thong tin conversation va thanh vien cua GROUP.
- Xoa conversation kem cac ban ghi member lien quan.
- Validate request truoc khi ghi database.
- Su dung transaction cho cac thao tac ghi de tranh tinh trang save conversation thanh cong nhung save member that bai.

2. Cac dependency moi trong service

- ConversationRepository: thao tac bang conversations.
- ConversationMemberRepository: thao tac bang conversation_user.
- UserRepository: kiem tra va lay User tu memberIds khi tao hoac them member.

Class duoc gan:

- @Service: danh dau day la Spring service.
- @Transactional(readOnly = true): mac dinh cac ham doc chay trong transaction read-only.
- Cac ham create/update/delete duoc gan @Transactional rieng de cho phep ghi database.

3. getAllConversation()

Chuc nang:

- Lay tat ca conversation trong database.
- Sap xep theo updatedAt giam dan, conversation moi cap nhat gan nhat nam dau danh sach.
- Map tung Conversation sang ConversationSummaryResponse bang ConversationMapper.toSummary().

Cach hoat dong:

1. Goi conversationRepository.findAll(Sort.by(Sort.Direction.DESC, "updatedAt")).
2. Tao List<ConversationSummaryResponse>.
3. Lap qua tung Conversation va convert sang summary response.
4. Tra ve danh sach summary cho controller.

Bug/thieu sot da sua:

- Truoc do findAll() khong sort, danh sach chat co the khong theo thu tu moi nhat.
- Controller truoc do khai bao sai kieu tra ve List<ConversationResponse> trong khi service tra List<ConversationSummaryResponse>.

4. getConversationById(Long id)

Chuc nang:

- Lay chi tiet conversation theo id.
- Tra ve day du thong tin conversation kem danh sach member.

Cach hoat dong:

1. Goi findConversationOrThrow(id) de kiem tra id hop le va conversation ton tai.
2. Goi getMemberById(id) de lay member cua conversation.
3. Goi ConversationMapper.toResponse(conv, members).
4. Tra ve ConversationResponse.

Bug/thieu sot da sua:

- Truoc do logic check not found nam truc tiep trong method, hien da tach thanh helper findConversationOrThrow() de tai su dung cho get/update/delete.
- Van con gioi han: chua check user hien tai co phai member cua conversation hay khong, vi project chua co luong lay current user ro rang trong service.

5. createConversation(ConversationCreateRequest request)

Chuc nang:

- Tao moi conversation PRIVATE hoac GROUP.
- Tao cac ban ghi ConversationMember tu memberIds.
- Gan role ADMIN cho member dau tien neu la GROUP.
- Gan role MEMBER cho cac member con lai.
- Chan tao PRIVATE conversation trung giua cung 2 user.

Cach hoat dong:

1. Kiem tra request khac null.
2. Kiem tra request.type() khac null.
3. Lay va validate memberIds bang requireMemberIds().
4. Validate so luong member theo type:
   - PRIVATE phai co dung 2 member.
   - GROUP phai co it nhat 2 member.
5. Normalize name bang normalizeText().
6. Neu type la GROUP thi name bat buoc khong duoc blank.
7. Neu type la PRIVATE thi goi hasPrivateConversationWithMembers() de chan tao conversation trung.
8. Goi findUsersOrThrow(memberIds) de dam bao tat ca user id ton tai.
9. Convert request thanh Conversation bang ConversationMapper.requestToEntity().
10. Set lai name va image da trim/null hoa.
11. Save Conversation truoc de co convId.
12. Tao danh sach ConversationMember:
    - GROUP va member dau tien: ADMIN.
    - Cac truong hop con lai: MEMBER.
13. Save tat ca member bang conversationMemberRepository.saveAll().
14. Lay lai member theo convId va tra ve ConversationResponse.

Bug/thieu sot da sua:

- Truoc do createConversation chi save Conversation, khong tao member nen conversation tao ra bi rong thanh vien.
- Truoc do khong validate type/memberIds/name.
- Truoc do khong check user id co ton tai hay khong.
- Truoc do khong chan duplicate private conversation.
- Truoc do khong co transaction, co nguy co du lieu nua voi neu save conversation xong nhung save member that bai.

6. updateConversationById(Long id, ConversationUpdateRequest request)

Chuc nang:

- Cap nhat name va image cua conversation.
- Them member vao GROUP.
- Xoa member khoi GROUP.
- Khong cho them/xoa member cua PRIVATE conversation.
- Dam bao GROUP sau khi update van co it nhat 2 member.

Cach hoat dong:

1. Kiem tra request khac null.
2. Goi findConversationOrThrow(id) de lay conversation.
3. Neu request.name() khac null:
   - Trim bang normalizeText().
   - Neu sau trim thanh null thi bao loi "Conversation name cannot be blank".
   - Neu hop le thi setName().
4. Neu request.imgUrl() khac null:
   - Trim/null hoa bang normalizeText().
   - Set vao conversation.image.
5. Normalize addMemberIds va removeMemberIds bang normalizeMemberIds().
6. Neu co add/remove member thi goi updateMembers().
7. Save conversation.
8. Tra ve ConversationResponse moi nhat kem danh sach member.

Bug/thieu sot da sua:

- Truoc do updateConversationById de rong, khong tra ve du lieu va khong compile an toan ve logic.
- Them validate blank name.
- Them validate duplicate id/null id trong addMemberIds/removeMemberIds.
- Them chan add va remove cung mot user trong cung request.
- Them check user can add co ton tai.
- Them check user can remove co phai member hien tai hay khong.

- Xoa member truoc de tranh loi rang buoc khoa ngoai voi bang conversation_user.

9. findConversationOrThrow(Long id)

Chuc nang:

- Helper dung chung de lay Conversation theo id.
- Neu id null thi bao BadRequestException.
- Neu khong tim thay conversation thi bao ResourceNotFoundException.

Cach hoat dong:

1. Kiem tra id == null.
2. Goi conversationRepository.findById(id).
3. Neu Optional empty thi throw ResourceNotFoundException.
4. Neu co thi tra ve Conversation.

Chuc nang:

2. Neu co du lieu thi goi normalizeMemberIds() de check null/duplicate va giu thu tu.

11. normalizeMemberIds(List<Long> memberIds, String fieldName)

Chuc nang:

Cach hoat dong:

1. Neu list null thi tra ve List.of().
2. Lap qua tung id:
   - Neu id null thi throw BadRequestException.
   - Neu id bi lap thi throw BadRequestException.
3. Tra ve ArrayList moi tu LinkedHashSet.

14. hasPrivateConversationWithMembers(List<Long> memberIds)

Chuc nang:

- Kiem tra da ton tai PRIVATE conversation giua dung 2 user hay chua.

Cach hoat dong:

1. Tao Set targetIds tu memberIds.
2. Tim cac ConversationMember co userId nam trong targetIds.
3. Loc chi cac member thuoc conversation type PRIVATE.
4. Group theo convId.
5. Gom set userId cua tung conversation PRIVATE.
6. Neu co conversation nao co set userId bang dung targetIds thi tra true.
7. Neu khong co thi tra false.

Bug/thieu sot da sua:

- Truoc do co the tao nhieu private chat trung lap giua cung hai user.

15. updateMembers(Conversation conversation, List<Long> addMemberIds, List<Long> removeMemberIds)

Chuc nang:

- Xu ly them/xoa member cho GROUP conversation.
- Dam bao du lieu thanh vien hop le sau update.

Cach hoat dong:

1. Neu conversation la PRIVATE thi throw BadRequestException.
2. Kiem tra overlap giua addMemberIds va removeMemberIds:
   - Neu cung mot user vua add vua remove thi throw BadRequestException.
3. Lay current members cua conversation.
4. Tao Set currentMemberIds.
5. Kiem tra removeMemberIds:
   - Neu user khong nam trong currentMemberIds thi throw ResourceNotFoundException.
6. Kiem tra addMemberIds:
   - Neu user da co trong currentMemberIds thi throw DuplicateResourceException.
7. Tinh nextMemberIds sau khi remove va add.
8. Neu GROUP con duoi 2 member thi throw BadRequestException.
9. Goi findUsersOrThrow(addMemberIds) de dam bao user can add ton tai.
10. Xoa cac member trong removeMemberIds bang deleteAllById().
11. Tao ConversationMember moi cho cac user can add voi role MEMBER.
12. Save cac member moi bang saveAll().
13. Goi ensureGroupHasAdmin() de dam bao group van co admin.

16. ensureGroupHasAdmin(Long conversationId)

Chuc nang:

- Dam bao GROUP van co it nhat mot ADMIN sau khi update member.
- Truong hop admin cu bi xoa, member dau tien con lai se duoc gan ADMIN.

Cach hoat dong:

1. Lay danh sach members hien tai.
2. Kiem tra co member nao role ADMIN khong.
3. Neu khong co admin va list khong rong:
   - Lay member dau tien.
   - Set role ADMIN.
   - Save member do.

- Neu co noi dung thi trim() va tra ve chuoi da cat khoang trang dau/cuoi.

18. Cac bug compile va mapping da sua ngoai service

- MemberResponse truoc do la record rong, trong khi MemberMapper tao object voi 6 tham so. Da them cac field:
  userId, username, displayName, avatarUrl, online, role.
- MemberMapper truoc do thieu import java.util.List. Da them import.
- ConversationController truoc do getAllConversations khai bao List<ConversationResponse> nhung service tra List<ConversationSummaryResponse>. Da doi controller ve List<ConversationSummaryResponse>.
- ConversationService.deleteConversationById truoc do tra ConversationResponse, nhung controller DELETE khong dung response. Da doi thanh void.
- ConversationMember truoc do thieu constructor/setter can thiet de service tao member moi. Da them no-arg constructor, constructor co Conversation/User/Role, va setter cho id/conversation/user/joinedAt.
- ConversationMemberId truoc do thieu no-arg constructor, JPA can constructor rong cho embeddable id. Da them constructor rong.
- ConversationMapper truoc do cast khong can thiet sang List<MemberResponse>. Da goi truc tiep MemberMapper.toResponseList().
- ConversationMemberRepository duoc bo sung findByUser_UserIdIn(Collection<Long> userIds) de phuc vu check duplicate private conversation.
